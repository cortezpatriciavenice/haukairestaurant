The Haukai Restaurant

Home to traditional Maori kai.

The Haukai Restaurant is a newly established eatery in the Bay of Islands. We provide a traditional and authentic dining experience with the fusion of Māori and Pākeha food/kai. Our kitchen uses only the freshest produce. Sourced from the local area when preparing each and every meal. We provide wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages to cater for every family member. We have a selection of indoor and outdoor seating available. Whether you're looking for a family gathering out or a romantic night out.
